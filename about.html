About Us

Answers Business Services’ vision of empowering businesses by making registration and compliance accessible and straightforward. Our mission is to support Ghanaian entrepreneurs and organizations with trusted, efficient services.

At Answers Business Services, we specialize in streamlining business registration processes for entrepreneurs and companies across Ghana. Our dedicated team assists with a wide array of registration-related services, such as:

  1. Renewing sole proprietorships
  2. Amending company information
  3. Filing annual returns
  4. Reprinting lost documents
  5. Offering consultancy to help startups grow

With a commitment to customer satisfaction and efficiency, we make it easy for clients to establish and expand their businesses, freeing them from complex administrative processes.
